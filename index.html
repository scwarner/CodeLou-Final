Welcome

The Louisville Mandolin Orchestra is an acoustic ensemble of mandolins, mandolas, mandocellos, guitars and bass performing instrumental music from early baroque and renaissance to classical and modern. Since 1988, our mission has been to promote community music-making and provide performance opportunities to players of all abilities.

The Louisville Mandolin Orchestra is a private, all-volunteer organization that relies partially on individual and business support to operate.
